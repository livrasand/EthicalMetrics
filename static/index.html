How EthicalMetrics Works

Get started in 3 simple steps. No credit card required.

1

Create Your Account

Register at /nuevo.html to get your unique site_id and admin_token.

POST /nuevo
{
  "site_id": "EM_ABC123",
  "admin_token": "em_xyz789"
}
2

Add Tracking Script

Include our lightweight script in your website's <head>:

<script src="https://ethicalmetrics.io/em.js" 
        defer 
        data-site-id="YOUR_SITE_ID">
</script>
<body data-modulo="home">
  <!-- Your content -->
  <script src="https://ethicalmetrics.io/em.js" defer></script>
</body>
3

View Your Dashboard

Access your private analytics dashboard with your credentials:

https://ethicalmetrics.io/dashboard ?site=YOUR_SITE_ID&token=YOUR_ADMIN_TOKEN
