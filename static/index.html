How EthicalMetrics Works

Get started in 3 simple steps. No credit card required.

1

Create your account

Regístrate fácil y rápido, sin correos, sin contraseñas y sin KYC.

{
  "site_id": "EM_ABC123",
  "admin_token": "em_xyz789"
}
2

Add EM Script

Include our lightweight script in your website's <head>:

<script src="https://ethicalmetrics.onrender.com/ethicalmetrics.js?id=YOUR_SITE_ID">
</script>
<body data-modulo="home">
  ...
  <script src="https://ethicalmetrics.onrender.com/ethicalmetrics.js?id=YOUR_SITE_ID">
</script>
</body>

View your analytics

Access your private analytics dashboard with your credentials:
